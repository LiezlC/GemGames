The Ascent

A contemplative pilgrimage binding fourteen interactive shrine-applets into a single climb. You begin at the trailhead at dusk; as you ascend, the night deepens, thins, and finally breaks into dawn at the summit. Each station is a self-contained interactive piece — enter it, sit with it, and light its lantern when you are ready to move on.

Provenance. The interactive applets in this collection were originally generated by Google Gemini from source images. They were curated, consolidated, and extended into this pilgrimage by Liezl Coetzee. The source images below are the seeds each shrine grew from.
