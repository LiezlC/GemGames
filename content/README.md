## 🔗 How It Works

Each theme folder contains **symbolic links** to games in the main `extracted/` directory. This means:

- ✅ Games can appear in multiple themes
- ✅ No file duplication (saves space)
- ✅ Updates to games automatically reflect in all categories
- ✅ Both organizational systems work independently

## 📊 Dual Organization

Your games are now organized in **two complementary ways**:

1. **By Gameplay Type** (`extracted/` folder)
   - action/ - Fast-paced reflex games
   - puzzle/ - Logic and problem-solving
   - simulation/ - Strategy and management
   - memory/ - Pattern matching
   - rhythm/ - Music and timing
   - sonic/ - Games with audio
   - visual/ - Visual experiences
   - adventure/ - Story-driven

2. **By Theme** (`content/` folder - this folder!)
   - AI-Aware - AI consciousness themes
   - Cosmo_Spacer - Space and cosmic
   - Neon_Cyber - Cyberpunk aesthetics
   - Nature_Harmony - Natural environments
   - Dragons - Mythical creatures
   - Mystic_Rituals - Spiritual journeys
   - Farmy_Fun - Agricultural themes
   - Blurring_Boundaries - Human-AI fusion
   - HouseSim - Architecture & home design
   - Light_Patterns - Luminescence & illumination
   - Paint_Splash - Artistic expression
   - UX_Landings - Interface design
   - Us_vs_Them - Competitive conflict
   - Zazen - Meditation & zen
